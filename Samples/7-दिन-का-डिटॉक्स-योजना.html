Frequently Asked Questions

Can I follow this if I have a medical condition?

It's a general plan designed for overall wellness, but we strongly advise checking with your doctor before starting if you have any serious health issues, diabetes, heart conditions, or are pregnant/nursing. The detox is gentle but may not be suitable for all medical conditions.

Is this suitable for vegetarians?

Yes! Our recipe bundle includes both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. The vegetarian recipes are clearly marked, and you can follow the entire detox plan with just vegetarian meals. We've ensured that vegetarian options are nutritionally complete with adequate protein sources.

Is it safe for beginners or working women?

Absolutely! The plan is specifically designed with busy lifestyles in mind. All recipes use everyday Indian ingredients and most can be prepared in under 30 minutes. The detox is gentle and focuses on nourishing your body rather than restrictive dieting. We've included meal prep tips for working professionals to make healthy eating convenient.
